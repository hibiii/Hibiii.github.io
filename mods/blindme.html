A client-sided mod that gives you the Blindness (or Darkness) effect all the time. Configurable per world and server.

Currently a beta version, there might be some bugs.

By default, new worlds and servers don't have any effects turned on. To change this, use the /blindme command, or use the settings screen, accessible through Mod Menu or through the /blindme settings subcommand.

Requirements

  • Quilt Loader or compatible
  • Quilt Standard Libraries on Modrinth or compatible
  • (Optional) Mod Menu on CurseForge and on Modrinth or compatible
